Privacy Policy for SDQ Digital Signature Solution

Last updated: 09/19/2024

Important Notice: The SDQ Digital Signature Solution is designed for use by authorized individuals within client companies. User accounts are created and managed by company administrators, not through public sign-up.

SDQ is committed to protecting your privacy while providing a secure and efficient Digital Signature Solution. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our SDQ Digital Signature Solution mobile app for creating, managing, and verifying digital signatures on documents.

1. Information We Handle in Our Digital Signature Solution

Our Digital Signature Solution processes the following types of information:

  • User account information (managed by your company administrator)
  • Documents and files you upload or sign using our Digital Signature Solution
  • Digital signatures you create, including signature metadata and timestamps
  • Document workflow information, including approvals and rejections
  • Usage data related to your interactions with the Digital Signature Solution

2. How We Use Your Information in Our Digital Signature Solution

We use the information to:

  • Provide and maintain the core functionality of our Digital Signature Solution, including signature creation, verification, and document management
  • Process and manage digital signature workflows, ensuring the integrity and authenticity of signed documents
  • Provide a more convenient user experience by saving and pre-filling form data for future uploads
  • Authenticate your access and manage permissions within the Digital Signature Solution
  • Store user input data (Upload Form) to improve your experience and ensure ease of use. For example, we may save the last file configuration you entered to make future uploads faster and more convenient.
  • Process file uploads, including PDFs and images, securely, to ensure they are properly stored and can be accessed for further use or editing.
  • Enable profile editing, including changing your profile picture, which is stored securely to reflect changes made within your profile settings.
  • Improve the performance and user experience of our Digital Signature Solution
  • Comply with legal obligations related to digital signatures and enforce our terms of service

3. Information Sharing and Storage in Our Digital Signature Solution

Your information within our Digital Signature Solution:

  • Is stored securely on our servers with encryption to protect the integrity of your digital signatures and documents
  • May be shared with other authorized users within your company as necessary for document signing workflows
  • Is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes
  • May be accessed by our service providers who assist in operating and maintaining our Digital Signature Solution, under strict confidentiality agreements
  • May be disclosed if required by law or to respond to legal process, particularly in matters related to the validity of digital signatures

4. Data Security in Our Digital Signature Solution

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data and ensure the integrity of digital signatures, including encryption of data in transit and at rest, secure key management, and tamper-evident storage of signed documents. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices in Our Digital Signature Solution

To exercise your rights regarding your personal data within our Digital Signature Solution, please contact your company administrator. They can assist with:

  • Accessing, correcting, or deleting your information
  • Objecting to or restricting the processing of your data
  • Requesting a copy of your data and signature history in a portable format
  • Managing your signature preferences and defaults
  • Clearing saved form data within the app settings

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our Digital Signature Solution. We will notify users of any significant changes through the app and update the "Last updated" date on this page.
