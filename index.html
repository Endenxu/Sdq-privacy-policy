Privacy Policy for SDQ App

Last updated: 09/12/2024

SDQ is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our Digital Signature Solution application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and company affiliation.
  • User Content: Files uploaded for digital signature, including PDFs and associated metadata.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you use the App, including file uploads, approvals, and digital signatures.
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, and browser type.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality of digital signature management.
  • Process and store uploaded files for signature and approval workflows.
  • Authenticate users and manage access permissions within company accounts.
  • Send notifications related to file status, approvals, and signatures.
  • Improve and optimize the App's performance and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

3. Sharing of Your Information

We share your information in the following ways:

  • With other authorized users within your company account, as necessary for the App's functionality.
  • With service providers who assist us in operating, maintaining, and improving the App.
  • If required by law or to respond to legal process.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption of data in transit and at rest. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data.
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
